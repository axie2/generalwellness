  Benefits of Weightlifting

   Contrary to popular belief, weightlifting isn't only about building muscle. Some of its benefits are:

  • Better sleep
  • Improved posture
  • Boosting metabolism
  • Burning body fat
  • Strengthening bones and joints
  • Reducing injury risks
  • Improving heart health

 If you're a beginner weightlifter, it is important for you to take it slow, and increase weight as you get stronger. Remember to be mindful of your form and take the necessary rest days.

Benefits of Cardio

Cardio isn't only good for weight loss, there are a ton of benefits that come from it

  • Strengthens your heart
  • Reduces the risk of several diseases
  • Improves lung capacity
  • Helps the immune system
  • Improves sleep quality
